BlueBrick
 Version 1.6.1

 by Alban Nanty

BlueBrick is a free software for Windows/Vista developped by Alban Nanty in open source. You can use it for your personnal purpose but not for commercial profit. This program is distributed in the hope that it will be useful, but WITHOUT ANY WARRANTY.

This software was designed specially for the AFOLs who want to prepare the layouts of their LEGO® exhibitions. BlueBrick is compatible with LDRAW and Train Depot Track Designer and was designed to extend easily its part database. Its layers feature allow you to better organize your map, and some specific layers make possible the addition of annotation and area assignment.
