BlueBrick
 Version 1.9.0

 by Alban Nanty

BlueBrick is a free software developed by Alban Nanty in open source which can run on Windows (with the Dot Net framework) or on Linux or MacOS (with the Mono framework or other framework). You can use it for your personal purpose but not for commercial profit. This program is distributed in the hope that it will be useful, but WITHOUT ANY WARRANTY.

This software was designed specially for the AFOLs who want to prepare the layouts of their LEGO® exhibitions. BlueBrick is compatible with LDRAW, Train Depot Track Designer and 4DBrix nControl and was designed to extend easily its part database. Its layers feature allow you to better organize your map, and some specific layers make possible the addition of annotation, rulers and area assignment.
