Appearance Preferences


Display Free Connection Points toggles the various connection indicators on or off. They are on in the default setting to help indicate available connection points, or broken links between parts. You may wish to switch this option off when you have completed your layout.

The default Color Scheme is "Blue Print" You can also select a Parchment or Classic version. Custom color schemes can be made by selecting "Custom" from drop down list and choosing your colors by clicking on the color boxes next to the following text entries. "Background Color", "Default Grid Color" and "Default sub-grid Color". The result of your color selection will be shown in the preview window. The changes will not take effect until after press "Ok" in the BlueBrick Options window.
